Department of Anatomy · Omandurar Medical College · Chennai

Reflection

Being a part of Anatomy Day was both exciting and eye-opening. The CMEs introduced me to perspectives of anatomy I hadn't encountered before, making learning feel dynamic and meaningful beyond the classroom. I'm truly grateful for the opportunity to have participated. Experiences like this remind me why I chose medicine.
